Annex D1 – General procurement

Justification for the award of the contract without prior publication of a contract notice in the Official Journal of the European Union (OJEU)
Directive 2004/18/EC

The Council uses a number of components from the ESRI suite of products to support its Geographical Information System (GIS) Strategy, these components support a number of key solutions across the Council including the Electronic Service Delivery platform, the corporate website (www.leeds.gov.uk), Development Control, Environmental Action Services, Highways Maintenance and the Local Land and Property Gazetteer. The ESRI products are proprietary and therefore support and maintenance can only be provided by ESRI UK Ltd.
