Notice Type: Contract Notice
Has Documents: No
Abstract:

Refuse and waste related services. South and City College Birmingham (SCCB) is a large further educational college delivering a high quality further education provision at 10 sites located from the centre of Birmingham to approximately 10 miles south of the City. We are looking for an organization to supply waste and removal services across all sites. Waste materials include general waste such as paper and cardboard as well as specialist waste such as building construction materials. The supplier will be expected to deliver an environmentally sound and compliant service in line with current evolving legislation.
