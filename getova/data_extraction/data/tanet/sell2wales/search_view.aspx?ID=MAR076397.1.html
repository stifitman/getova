2 Contract Details

2.1

Title

Travel Trade Services For The German Speaking Markets

2.2

Description of the goods or services required

Visit Wales contributes to the Welsh Government’s strategic and operational targets designed to grow the visitor economy in Wales. The key action being to promote Wales as a destination with a high quality tourism offer.

The Welsh Government is seeking to appoint a contractor to contribute towards the overall growth target for tourism to Wales and deliver an effective and dynamic on territory travel trade service. The aim is to drive awareness of Wales as a holiday destination, educate the travel trade industry on the range of products and experiences available in Wales, and identify and develop partnerships with key players in the travel trade industry to pursue new and further opportunities to increase both the awareness of and number of visits to Wales.

NOTE: The authority is using eTenderwales to carry out this procurement process. To obtain further information record your interest on Sell2Wales at http://www.sell2wales.gov.uk/search/search_switch.aspx?ID=24415
