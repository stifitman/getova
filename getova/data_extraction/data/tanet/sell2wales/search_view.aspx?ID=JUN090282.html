Title: Waste Haulage Framework.
Published by: Redcar and Cleveland Borough Council
Publication Date: 27/06/2015
Application Deadline:
Deadline Date:

Notice Type: Contract Award Notice
Has Documents: No
Abstract:

The Council required providers to transport waste via methods including walking floor/ejector articulated trailers. The successful organisation will supply their own vehicles for the transportation which involves the collection of waste from nominated sites and delivery to nominated sites on an as and when basis. This is a framework of up to 4 years.
