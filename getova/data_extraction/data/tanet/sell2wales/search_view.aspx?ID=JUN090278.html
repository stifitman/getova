Title: Media Monitoring and Evaluation and Related Services.
Published by: The Minister for the Cabinet Office acting through Crown Commercial Service
Publication Date: 27/06/2015
Application Deadline:
Deadline Date:
Deadline Time:
Notice Type: Contract Award Notice
Has Documents: No
Abstract:

Crown Commercial Service as the Contracting Authority awarded a Framework Agreement that is for use by UK public sector bodies (and any future successors to these organisations), identified at section VI.3 of the Contract Notice, that have a need for media monitoring and evaluation and related services.

The Services within the scope of this Framework Agreement are sub-divided into 2 Lots:

Lot 1: A multi-Supplier Lot for the provision of media monitoring and evaluation and related services;

Lot 2: A multi-Supplier Lot for the provision of an online social media monitoring and analysis tool.
