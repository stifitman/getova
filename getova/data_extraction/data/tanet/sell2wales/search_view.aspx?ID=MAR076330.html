Title: 508 Chiller Replacement.
Published by: Birmingham Airport
Publication Date: 17/03/2015
Application Deadline: 31/03/2015
Deadline Date: 01/04/2015
Deadline Time: 11:00:00
Notice Type: Contract Notice
Has Documents: No
Abstract:

Appointment of a contractor is required to replace the existing North Terminal water chillers and associated cooling towers with high efficiency air cooled chillers. Shortlisted contractors are to procure and install the chillers and associated pipework based upon design guidance provided by Birmingham Airport. The contractor is to provide ongoing maintenance of the system. There is also the potential requirement for the replacement of the AHU within the Departure Lounge, as identified under lot 2. PQQ documentation is to be obtained via the Intend website by completion of the supplier registration process: https://in-tendhost.co.uk/birminghamairport/aspx/Home

II.2)

Quantity or Scope of the Contract

II.2.1)

Total quantity or scope

The 4 existing water cooled chillers and the cooling towers are to be removed and replaced with high efficiency air cooled chillers (Lot 1).

It is anticipated that the new chillers will be located on the roof of the energy building. Due to the relocation of the chillers the successful contractor will be required to route the primary chilled water pipework from the new chiller location to connect into the existing chilled water system.

The contractor will be required to provide ongoing maintenance.

Within this scope of work the Airport may also require the contractor to replace AHU within the departure lounge. Lot 2 is therefore for the replacement of the chillers and the AHU replacement.

VI.3)

Additional Information

In order to access the PQQ documentation please follow the process as outlined here:

1. Click on the hyperlink in the OJEU notice. https://in-tendhost.co.uk/birminghamairport/aspx/Home

2. If you are not registered with In-Tend through Birmingham Airport. You will be required to complete all of the fields in yellow.

3. Once this has been done then click on the register my company' tab. Your company is now registered.

4. Click Home' tab.

5. Login to the system by entering your e-mail address and password.

6. Highlight tender' tab.

7. Click on current' tab.

8. Find the relevant PQQ that you are interested in accessing.

9. Click view details' tab.

10. Click on PQQ' tab.

11. Click on download' tab.

12. You will need to complete the questionnaire off-line.

13. In order to submit your PQQ response you will need to login to the system again (step 5) and follow the process as above through to step 10.

14. In order to submit your PQQ response click on attach document' tab.

15. Once you are content that all of your documents have been attached click on the red submit return' tab.

Lodging of appeals


Birmingham Airport will incorporate a standstill period at the point information on the award of the contract is communicated to tenderers. That notification will provide full information on the award decision. The standstill period, which will be for a minimum of 10 calendar days, provides time for unsuccessful tenderers to challenge the award decision before the contract is entered into.

The Utilities Contracts Regulations 2006 (SI 2006 No 6) provide for aggrieved parties who have been harmed or are at risk of harm by a breach of the rules to take action in the High Court (England, Wales and Northern Ireland).
