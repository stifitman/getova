Abstract:

Scottish Water Scientific Services is required to deliver a programme of analysis arising from the Chemical Investigations programme in Scotland between 2015 and 2017 to understand the impact of priority and priority hazardous substances from sewage treatment works on the environment. The requirements are as follows (note this may be subject to change and is based on current information):

b) River water quality analysis up and downstream of the waste water treatment works listed under for priority hazardous substances, priority substances and specific pollutants and pharmaceuticals

II.2)

Quantity or Scope of the Contract

II.2.1)

Total quantity or scope

Scottish Water Scientific Services is required to deliver a programme of analysis arising from the Chemical Investigations programme in Scotland between 2015 and 2017 to understand the impact of priority and priority hazardous substances from sewage treatment works on the environment. The requirements are as follows (note this may be subject to change and is based on current information):

a) Wastewater treatment works influent and final effluent monitoring for priority hazardous substances, priority substances and specific pollutants and pharmaceuticals.

b) River water quality analysis up and downstream of the waste water treatment works listed under for priority hazardous substances, priority substances and specific pollutants and pharmaceuticals

c) Wastewater treatment works sludge monitoring for priority hazardous substances, priority substances and specific pollutants and pharmaceuticals.

Participating laboratories must meet the analytical performance requirements of the UK CIP2 Technical Working Group on sampling and analysis.

The analysis requirement is as follows (note this may be subject to change and is based on current information available):

— 480 final effluent samples to be analysed over a 2 year period at 20 wastewater treatment works;

— 480 influent samples to be analysed over a 2 year period at 20 wastewater treatment works;

— 960 river water samples to be analysed over a 2 year period from upstream and downstream of 20 wastewater treatment works;

— additional programme of analysis for process optimisation to be defined;

— additional programme of Sludge analysis to be defined.
