Title: Dynamic Purchasing System for Local and Home to School Transport — Further Competition.
Published by: North Lincolnshire Borough Council
Publication Date: 27/06/2015
Application Deadline:
Deadline Date: 09/07/2015
Deadline Time: 12:00:00
Notice Type: Contract Notice
Has Documents: No
Abstract:

North Lincolnshire Borough Council is inviting PSV operators to submit an indicative tender to be included on a dynamic purchasing system (DPS) relating to the provision of local and home to school bus Services (OJEU Ref: 2015/S 021-034248). North Lincolnshire Council is seeking to go out to further competition under the Dynamic Purchasing System for a number of home to school bus contracts on behalf of John Leggott College (Address: West Common Lane, Scunthorpe, North Lincolnshire, DN17 1DS) with an estimated value of c. 1 000 000 GBP, and as such any operator not yet submitted to the dynamic purchasing system may apply by submitting an indicative tender no later than 12:00 noon 9.7.2015. To be included on the dynamic purchasing system (DPS) operators will have to satisfy the council that they meet minimum quality standards as set out in the indicative tender documentation. Interested parties must download the indicative tender document and upload completed via YORtender http://www.yortender.co.uk no later than 12 noon 9.7.2015. The council will then invite all operators on the Dynamic Purchasing System to submit bids for the contracts.

The council reserves the right to utilise e-Auctions for the further competition stage.

The contracting authority in this instance will be John Leggott College.
