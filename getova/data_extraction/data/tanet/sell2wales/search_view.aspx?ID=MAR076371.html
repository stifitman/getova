Nitrogen High Pressure PTFE Hose Test Facility

2.2

Description of the goods or services required

To design & develop a high pressure testing facility (6500 psi) with a air and nitrogen testing for our PTFE hoses, ranging in sizes Ø¼” to Ø2”. To will give us the capability to manufacture, test and supply fully certified pressure tested PTFE hoses.
