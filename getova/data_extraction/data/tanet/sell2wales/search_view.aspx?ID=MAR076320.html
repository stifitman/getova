Title: Development and Operation of a business Growth Hub in the North East Local Enterprise Partnership Ar
Published by: Sunderland City Council
Publication Date: 17/03/2015
Application Deadline:
Deadline Date:
Deadline Time:
Notice Type: Contract Award Notice
Has Documents: No
Abstract:

Sunderland City Council on behalf of the North East Local Enterprise Partnership was seeking to appoint a partner to operate a business Growth Hub' for the North East LEP area. In keeping with national government requirements as articulated by BIS and as part of the emerging national network of Growth Hubs, the North East Growth Hub will enable businesses in the LEP area to access the business and enterprise support and access to finance advice they need to start, sustain and grow their business activities.

The contract comprises of two components:

1) development of a digital platform (preferably utilising software available in the market which has the capacity for development); and

2) operation and management of the North East Growth Hub.

The North East Growth Hub will provide a range of functions. The successful bidder will manage the development and delivery of each function within the agreed contract period.

The successful bidder will be required to produce a business case for the operation of the North East Growth Hub for a 5 year period beyond the contract term.

The contract will be for 16 months until 30.6.2016. The Council reserves the right to extend on an annual basis the development and support maintenance of the awarded solution. This is subject to resourcing and at the sole discretion of the NELEP.
