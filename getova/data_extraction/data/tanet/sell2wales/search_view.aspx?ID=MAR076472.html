Title

Provision of Laundry Services to Aberystwyth University

2.2

Description of the goods or services required

Aberystwyth University Residential & Hospitality Services are seeking supplier/s with all year linen provision, with an emphasis on the summer months. This tender will be broken down into 2 lots to separate the requirements of both sections. Companies will have an opportunity to bid for one or both lots. At present the laundering is out sourced. This external organisation has the right to express interest and bid for this procurement as they see fit.

Qualification envelope to be completed first - itt code 41880

Lot 1 is for a fully managed laundry service for linen - itt code 42114 refers

Lot 2 is a requirement for the supply and managed laundry services of Chefs uniforms, Table cloths, Oven cloths and Tea towels to be delivered and collected weekly. This is an all year round requirement for the department - itt code 42158 refers

2.4

Total quantity or scope of tender

This scope is for

Lot 1 - Fully managed laundry service. The University provides a service to 3,616 rooms during our conference season June - September annually. A very small proportion is also used during the Easter period, and infrequently during term time for the provision of emergency accommodation.

Lot 2 - Fully managed Laundry Service - Uniforms and Linen. Hospitality Services have the requirement for the supply of Chefs uniform, Table cloths and Oven cloths and Tea towels to be delivered and collected weekly. This is an all year round requirement for the department.
