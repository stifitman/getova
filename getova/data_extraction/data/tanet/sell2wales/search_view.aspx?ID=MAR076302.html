Title: Provision of Used Cars for Training Purposes.
Published by: Ministry of Defence
Publication Date: 17/03/2015
Application Deadline:
Deadline Date:
Deadline Time:
Notice Type: Contract Award Notice
Has Documents: No
Abstract:

Second-hand transport vehicles. The Authority has a requirement for the supply of used cars to enable training primarily at Kineton and occasionally at Bicester. The vehicles are to be supplied in a visually serviceable condition and are to include all doors, windows, boot, bonnet and internal fittings. They must be capable of being moved around on four wheels and include ignition keys. Estimated quantities are 40-50 per month at the Kineton site and 4-5 every 2 months at the Bicester site. The vehicles shall be delivered within a 48 hour notice period and collected within a 7 day notice period. The majority of vehicles will be returned with windows smashed and some interior damage and in some cases will be returned with the roof and other parts separated from the main chassis.

The MOD SID is a database of active and potential suppliers available to all MOD and UK Defence procurement personnel, and is the main supplier database of MOD Procurement organisations. Please note: the registration and publication of a company profile on the MOD SID does not mean or imply that the supplier has in any way been vetted or approved by the MOD.
