2 Contract Details

2.1

Title

Tender for supply of Analytical Chemistry Equipment

2.2

Description of the goods or services required

To assist in the analysis of organic and inorganic compounds and elements in various matrices, the analytical equipment including GC, IR, UV/Vis spectrophotometer, HPLC, Fluorometer and a Microwave Plasma-Atomic Emission Spectrometer have been identified as essential to the University’s research activities. The project is being funded by WEFO monies and Swansea University is seeking a real and substantial in-kind contribution from suppliers.
