Wales Centre for Behaviour Change (WCBC) Final Evaluation.

2.2

Description of the contract

The Wales Centre for Behaviour Change (WCBC) is a multi-disciplinary research unit based at Bangor University.

The Centre is part-funded by European Regional Development Funds (ERDF) through the Welsh Government for 2.5 years from 1st January 2013 to 30th June 2015.

Knowledge and innovation for growth. WCBC aims to promote economic prosperity in Small- and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) within the Convergence Region of Wales by implementing innovative and evidence-based behaviour change interventions, addressing, for example, wellbeing and psychological health and safety. Running through all the Centre projects is a cross-cutting theme of sustainability.

The project partners wish to commission an external evaluator to report on both the impact of the project and the sustainability of the Centre after the project end-date. Please see the attached Terms of Reference for full details.
